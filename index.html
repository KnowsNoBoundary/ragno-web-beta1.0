RAGNO

This name is something that took me a while to come up with... because I like to create meaningful names, such as this one. RAGNO means spider in Italian, and I thought it was cool to have an animal mascot such as a spider. I was also thinking on making a "Special Elite Team" such as DELTA Force! To those who are not aware of DELTA Force, it's a Special Elite Team that the USA has in hold for Special OPs. Which is cool if you ask me! The other part that I am proud of this name is the first 2 letters "RA". It's an Egyptian Sun God, and that's pretty cool as well. The last reason for me being proud of such name is, keeping the name in all caps, because of abbreviations! I haven't found one that we can use, but in time, I am sure I will find one! I could also just keep it like that, because RAGNO will not be a one game only. Therefore, the acronym for a specific game, will be different for another game. Anyways, that's all and I hope you can be proud of this name as well!
